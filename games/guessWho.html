How to Play: Who Am I? - The Profile Edition

  • Goal: Find out which character the other player has chosen.
  • Each player secretly chooses a character from the grid.
  • Each character has a profile: job, hobbies, likes, dislikes, birthplace, and a fun fact.
  • You can choose how many characters appear: 8, 12, or 16.
  • Take turns asking yes/no questions to learn about the mystery character.
  • Example questions: “Does your person like animals?”, “Is your person from Spain?”, “Does your person have a fun job?”
  • Use the buttons to show or hide parts of the character profile (job, hobbies, etc).
  • If the answer is "no", click on characters to mark them as eliminated.
  • Click “Switch Player” after each turn to change turns.
  • Click “Mark Eliminated” to remove characters that do not match the clues.
  • You win when you guess the other player's character correctly.
