Code Names Game

How to Play

  • Goal: Find all of your partner's words by giving good clues.
  • Each player has a list of secret words (you can see your words, but not your partner's).
  • Take turns: one gives a clue, the other guesses.
  • How to Give a Clue: Say one word and one number. The word should connect some of your partner's secret words. The number tells how many words match your clue.
  • How to Guess: Try to guess the correct words based on the clue. You can guess up to the number your partner said.
  • Example: Your partner's words are: Sun, Fire, Desert. You say: "Hot 3"
  • Keep taking turns until all of your partner's words are found!
