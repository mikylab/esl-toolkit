Sentence Relay Game

How to Play: Sentence Relay

  • Goal: Work together to make a funny or interesting story, one sentence at a time.
  • Click the button to get a starter sentence.
  • A grammar rule will also appear — try to follow it in your sentence.
  • Player 1 reads the sentence and adds a new sentence to continue the story.
  • Click "Submit Sentence" to add your sentence to the story.
  • Now it’s Player 2’s turn. Players take turns adding new sentences.
  • Each new sentence should follow the grammar rule and make sense in the story.
  • If you make a mistake, the game will give feedback to help you fix it.
  • Click "Clear Story" if you want to start the story again.
  • Click "Reset Game" to start over with a new sentence and rule.
  • Have fun and be creative!
