Apples to Apples Game

Welcome to Apples to Apples! Here's how the game works:

  • Each round, a Green prompt card is drawn.
  • Each group has seven Red Cards to choose from.
  • Each group selects a Red Card that they think best matches the Green prompt.
  • Groups must justify their choice and convince the judge (the teacher) that their card should win.
  • The judge then picks the best match from the selections.

Get ready for some funny combinations!
